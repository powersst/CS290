6 Equal-Width Columns

A layout with 3 major breakpoints that eventually results in six equally-distributed columns. On medium screens it becomes a two-column layout. Once more space becomes available the layout breaks into three columns. Finally, the layout displays all six columns side by side on screens that can accommodate them.

Considerations

  • Watch line length
  • Switch to six column layout when the screen is much wider with at least 4-5 words column width.
