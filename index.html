This is a description of my work in progress project.

I am thinking about creating a system that will allow people to find companies, businesses, and stores that support charity through profit sharing. Companies such as HydroFlask allow users to donate a percentage of their purchase price to a charity of their choice. I think this is an important idea because many people are unwilling to donate to charity but will often choose to help charity if there is no additional cost to themselves.
